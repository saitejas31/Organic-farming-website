Anjura super food

Description

  • This is a super food that has 2 products as ingredient - Dry Anjura and Honey.
  • Local Anjura (Fig) was costed at Rs. 20/KG which was not worthy to farmers.
  • It is grown more in Dry places.
  • The value addition is provided by Semi Drying the Fig in shaded area for 2 to 3 days
  • Dipping the dry fig with Honey in a proportion
  • Allowing the dipped fig to dry in shaded area
  • Repeating the dip and dry process for 7 to 8 times to ensure correct absorption by the Fig
  • Final product is packed and made ready to ship

    • Challenges

    • 1 : More time to dry

    • 2 : Maintain hygiene by avoiding the attraction of flies to Honey
